Higher normalists, education graduates, professionals from other areas, education specialists, people with master's degrees and doctorates interested in being part of the Colombian teaching can be presented.

The teachers who participate in this call will be evaluated through a test of basic skills and competencies, as well as through a psychotechnical test. According to the announcement, the basic skills and competences test is intended to assess the levels of knowledge of the discipline, abilities and skills, aptitudes that the applicants of the public merit contest demonstrate.

The test that will be applied to the applicants will evaluate: Mathematical reasoning, critical reading, Pedagogy, psychotechnical or behavioral and the area or position to which they aspire to be appointed.
